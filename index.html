Deadset

A short film by Aidan Dickens


About the Film

D

eadset is a New Zealand Short film. It was made in November 2013 by Filmmaker Aidan Otene Dickens, who wrote, directed and prodcued it. The film is about two characters that are trying to escape from their lives and what happenes when they meet. The themes of the film are escape and wish fulfillment, with the idea being that escape isn't always the answer. Deadset was made for the 2014 Tropfest Film competition, but due to it's adult themed content it was unable to be screeened. It has since been entered in over 40 film festivals worldwide, and has recently won it's first award, an Award of Merit from the Indie Fest Film Festival in California, USA.

Synopsis

M

arcus is driving along a country road when he sees Daria hitchhiking and picks her up. Through conversation we learn that they are both running away from something; Marcus from his controlling mother and Daria from her dark past as a prostitute. Daria is a Wiccan and is trying to find an ancient, mystical door that will open for the right person and grant them a single wish, but when it comes time for her to say goodbye to Marcus it becomes clear that maybe they share a common future as well. Daria leaves Marcus and enters a tunnel where the door is hidden. Using her magic she draws a door on the wall and begins a blood ritual that will open it; but it doesn't work. Was it all in her mind, or is there another chance to have her wish granted and escape the life she's been running from? That's when Marcus enters the tunnels and comes looking for her, to see if she needs help and certain they are kindred spirits. Emotionally drained and at the end of the rope, Daria does something unexpected, and that's where things start to go wrong.

About the Filmmaker

A

idan is a New Zealand Filmmaker currently living in Auckland, New Zealand. Aidan has been making short films and music videos since 2012 and in that time has made 10 short films, with more in the pipeline for 2014. Aidan completed a diploma in filmmaking at SAE Institute Auckland in 2012, where he made 4 short films and 5 music videos. After the course finished in September he went straight into a short filmmaking certificate course at the NZ Film Academy in Auckland. Aidan is a skilled filmmaker and has written, directed and produced 10 short films. He is also a highly skilled editor and edits all his films and music videos with Final Cut Pro X, as well as doing his own sound design and visual effects in Adobe After Effects. Aidan's short films have been featured in Film Festivals both in New Zealand and overseas. His film 'Dark Teachings' won an award of merit from the accolade film festival in 2013. Aidan is also a highly skilled musician and plays guitar and sings in his own band 'Illuminus', and mixed and produced both their albums. He is a trained sound engineer and completed a diploma in audio engineering from SAE in 2011.
